The Ridleys

These is the Ridleys

  • The Ridleys is a 4-piece Alternative Folk rock band from the Philippines.
  • They are known for their ability to tell stories through their music.
  • There are four members in this band, namely; Benly Manaligod (vocalist), Jan De Vera (Lead Guitarist), Joric Canlas (Bassist), and Bryant Ayes (drummer).
