Lola Amour

This is Lola Amour

  • Lola Amour is a Filipino rock band from Muntinlupa, Philippines that dabbles in the genres of modern rock, funk, and pop.
  • Their group name "Lola Amour" comes after the vocalist, Pio Dumayas' grandmother.
  • The famous known songs of Lola Amour is "Fallen" and "Raining in Manila".
